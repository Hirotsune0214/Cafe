  1. How does Doe Pass work
    once I sign up?

    Once you sign up for the program, you will receive a confirmation email. All you need to do is to show the email to our lovely barista, then they will provide you with a physical Doe Pass Card as a proof that you are a member of the Doe Pass program

  2. How do I get Doe Pass Exclusive Pricing for my coffee and drinks?

    We verify your status by checking physical Doe Pass card. You don't need to scan the card or anything. Just flash it out before you order, our barista will ring you up with the exclusive pricing for you! If you forgot to bring your card when you visit us, it is totally fine as well. Just tell barista your name and you own Doe Pass, they will do a quick check from our POS system, then everything will just work the same.

  3. How do I cancel the subscription if I no longer need the program?

    If you wish to cancel the subscription of Doe Pass, simply bring back the physical card to the shop. Once we receive the card, we will cancel the subscription for you from our end and refund the remaining period of subscription back to your card. *Due to limitation of our technological support, we are unable to offer permission to subscribers to stop subscription by themselves. We are working on finding the solution to optimize the experience. We apologize for any inconvenience that may occur to you.
